MemoMatch

This game is a simple memory matching game. Here's how to play:

  • Select the difficulty level from the dropdown (Easy, Medium, Hard).
  • Each difficulty level will give you a different number of card pairs to match.
  • Click on two cards to flip them over and see if they match.
  • If they match, they will stay flipped over; otherwise, they will flip back.
  • Your goal is to match all pairs in the shortest time and with the fewest moves.
  • When the game starts, a countdown timer will appear.
  • You must complete the tasks or challenges displayed on the screen within the given time limit.

Good luck and have fun!
